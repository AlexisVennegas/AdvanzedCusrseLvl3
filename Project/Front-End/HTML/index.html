Welcome!

FireGuardian is a specialized application designed for firefighters, providing personalized information and high-priority alerts within their assigned zones

Risk map

Discover areas categorized by our analysis system as low, medium, or high risk of wildfires. We use artificial intelligence technology and data analysis to assess risk accurately.

FIRMS

FIRMS provides global data on active wildfires derived from satellites and can be a valuable source for monitoring and managing fire situations.

Weather station

Stay informed about current weather conditions in each area. Fireguardian integrates with reliable data sources to provide you with updates on temperature, wind speed, and humidity—key elements to understand the risk of wildfires.

Alerts

Receive immediate alerts upon the detection of possible fire outbreaks. Our cutting-edge technology and advanced data analysis will keep you one step ahead for a quicker and more efficient response.
